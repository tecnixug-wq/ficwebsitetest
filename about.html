Who We Are

FIC: Beyond Cooperative

We are a community of like-minded individuals from St. Francis Chapel Makerere University, bound by a common goal of economic prosperity and spiritual transformation. Since 2010, we've empowered members through accessible financial services.

FIC is a multipurpose cooperative Society registered with the Ministry of Trade, Industry and Co-operatives (Reg. 9897/RCS). Our common bond is members of the Franciscan Community — Makerere University staff, students, parents or children who are members of St. Francis Chapel Makerere.

  • Officially registered cooperative (Reg. 9897/RCS)
  • Member savings protected and managed responsibly
  • Rooted in the Franciscan Community at Makerere University
  • Committed to economic prosperity and spiritual transformation
