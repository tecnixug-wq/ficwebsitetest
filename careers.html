Careers

Why Join FIC?

We're looking for talented, passionate individuals who want to grow with us. At FIC, you'll find opportunities to develop your skills, contribute to our mission, and be part of a thriving cooperative community.

Collaborative Culture

Work with a supportive team dedicated to member success and cooperative values.

Growth Opportunities

Develop skills and advance your career with professional training and mentorship.

Mission-Driven Work

Contribute to economic prosperity and spiritual transformation in our community.

Competitive Benefits

Enjoy competitive compensation and benefits packages designed for our team.

No Adverts Available

We currently do not have any open positions or internships available. Thank you for your interest in joining FIC!

If you'd like to be considered for future opportunities, please send your CV to info@ficsociety.org and we'll keep your information on file.

Application Process

1

Submit Your Application

Send your CV, cover letter, and any relevant certifications to info@ficsociety.org

2

Review & Screening

Our HR team reviews all applications and contacts qualified candidates for interviews

3

Interview

Participate in one or more interviews to showcase your skills and fit with our team

4

Offer & Onboarding

Selected candidates receive an offer and join our FIC family with comprehensive onboarding support
