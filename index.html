Our Values

Why Choose FIC?

Every decision we make is guided by your best interests. Here's what sets us apart.

Trusted

Registered with the Ministry of Trade, registered SACCO since 2010.

Community

Rooted in the Franciscan values of care, compassion, and community.

Competitive

Attractive interest rates on savings and affordable loan rates for members.

Fast Access

Quick loan processing with responses within 36 hours of application.

Member Centric

Profits returned to members as dividends at the end of each financial year.

Empowering

Regular training and financial literacy sessions for all members.
