Regular Savings Account

Upon becoming a member, you automatically sign up for a regular savings product. In this product you commit to make a minimum monthly savings deposit (amount resolved by AGM). All other deposits, withdrawals, loan repayments, and purchases of investment units flow through this account. This is your gateway for all funds received by FIC and through which you receive all benefits.

Mandatory for Members Monthly Deposits Flexible Access Member Dividends
Key Details:
  • Minimum: Resolved by AGM from time to time
  • Interest: Competitive rates approved by AGM
  • Gateway for all member transactions

Time Deposit Savings

Lock in your savings for a fixed term and enjoy higher interest rates than regular accounts. In this account your deposit is fixed for an agreed predetermined period and interest rate. Ideal for members with lump-sum funds they wish to grow without touching for a defined period.

Higher Interest Rates Fixed Terms 3-12 Months Guaranteed Returns
Key Details:
  • Minimum Period: 3 months
  • Maximum Period: 12 months
  • Interest Rate: Fixed at opening per Board policy
  • Negotiable minimum threshold

Target Savings Account

Save for a particular specified purpose with targeted withdrawals. Whether it's for a holiday, vehicle, property deposit, or any other goal, this account helps you stay disciplined. Withdrawals are allowed once every 90 days to keep you on track toward your goal.

Goal-Oriented 90-Day Withdrawal Cycle Disciplined Saving Purpose-Driven
Key Details:
  • Withdrawal Frequency: Once per 90 days
  • Extra Withdrawal Charge: Applied for withdrawals within 90 days
  • Penalty fees: As determined by Board
Your Goal. Your Timeline.

Investment Units

These are non-withdrawable savings made with FIC for long-term investment purposes. You give instructions in writing to transfer your ordinary savings into investment units. This product attracts a guaranteed return on investment as shall be approved by the AGM from time to time. Perfect for members thinking long-term.

Long-term Growth Non-Withdrawable Guaranteed Returns Wealth Building
Key Details:
  • Non-withdrawable for duration
  • Guaranteed return as approved by AGM
  • Written instruction required to transfer
  • Perfect for long-term wealth accumulation

Loan Guarantee Savings

This savings product forms part of the collateral for members who secure their loans with minimum required savings. It provides security for your loan while helping you continue building your savings safety net. As a borrower, maintaining this account is essential.

Loan Collateral Security Reserve For Borrowers Safety Net
Key Details:
  • Used as loan collateral
  • Minimum amounts determined by Board
  • Required for loan security
  • Protected interest earnings

Interest on Capital Savings

This is a mandatory savings product for all FIC members. Its primary purpose is to facilitate the payment of interest on capital that has been mobilized from members. This account helps distribute returns fairly across the membership base and ensures transparent capital management.

Mandatory for Members Capital Returns Interest Distribution Transparent
Key Details:
  • Required account for all members
  • Receives interest on capital mobilized
  • Transparent capital management tool
  • Automatic deposits per Board decisions

Save for Investment Units

Build toward long-term wealth with this non-withdrawable savings product designed specifically for purchasing investment units. During the year, you make regular deposits into this account, and by December 31st, these funds automatically convert into investment units. This account earns returns over and above ordinary savings.

Year-End Conversion Higher Returns Long-term Growth Automatic IU Purchase
Key Details:
  • Converts to Investment Units by Dec 31st
  • Earns premium returns above regular savings
  • Non-withdrawable during year
  • Builds long-term investment portfolio
